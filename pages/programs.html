Program Overview

Are you a girl aged 10–16 who wants to grow academically, improve your English, and start preparing for university early? “Sophia Girls’ Academy” Program 2026 is a no-cost virtual program that aims to offer academic support across a variety of subjects.

What You'll Learn

Mathematics

Learn fundamentals of Algebra, Geometry, Introduction to Calculus.

Physics

Learn fundamentals of Mechanics, Electrostatics, Magnetism, Quantum Physics Thermodynamics, etc.

Chemistry

Learn fundamentals of Inorganic Chemistry, Organic Chemistry, etc.

Communication Skills

Hosted for 7th to 12th graders.

UK Colleges' Application Consultations

Hosted for 10th to 12th graders. The program includes essay writing, portfolio review, and preparation.

English (for International students)

Improvement of reading comprehension and speaking fluency, and English proficiency level.

English (CIS Countries)

Hosted exclusively for CIS citizens. Aimed to improve English proficiency level.

Basic Technical Skills

Learn to use digital tools like Google Docs, Disk, Excel, etc.

Financial Literacy & Marketing

Learn fundamentals of financial literacy and marketing. Learn to promote, calculate, and distribute.

SAT Math

Hosted for 10th to 12th graders. Learn to solve mathematical problems SAT problems, use Desmos, and improve your score.
